About Neural Havoc Chronicles

I'm ReaperTK, an Australian Podcaster, Gamer and lighting developer for Neural Havoc. Neural Havoc Chronicles is an immersive podcast to give insight on Neural Havoc, an upcoming Web3 NFT third-person game. Each episode is a self-contained descent into techno-horror corrupted AIs that whisper in sleep, black-market firmware that edits emotions, and dreamscapes that rewrite reality. Framed as recovered logs, clandestine broadcasts, and classified testimonies, the series blends psychological dread with hard sci-fi detail, asking one central question - when technology can trespass inside the skull, where does human identity end - and what monsters are waiting on the other side?
